HOW WE WORK

How Flow Interio Works?

At Flow Interio, our streamlined 5-step process ensures a seamless journey from concept to completion.

01. Book An Appointment

This initial step involves the client meeting with a designer to discuss the project's goals, preferences, budget, and timeline.

02.Desgin Selection

After understanding the client's needs, the designer proposes one or more design concepts.This might include sketches, mood boards, or digital renderings.

03. Production begins at Factory

Once the design is finalized, the production phase starts. This could involve manufacturing custom pieces, ordering materials, or preparing elements needed for the project.

04.Installation At Site

With all pieces produced and ready, the next step is installing them at the designated site. This process can include assembling modular cabinets

05. Move- In

The final phase marks the completion of the project, where the client can move into the space or start using it as intended
